## Adding a maintainer

The current rule is: once a contributor has merged ≥5 high-quality PRs
across at least two activity families, the founding maintainer may propose
them as a maintainer. Maintainer changes are recorded in `GOVERNANCE.md`.

## Removing a maintainer

A maintainer who is inactive for 12 consecutive months (no merged PRs, no
review activity, no response to outreach) is moved to "emeritus" status.
Active maintainers may also resign at any time.
